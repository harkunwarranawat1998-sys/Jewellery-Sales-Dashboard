📊 Production Sales Dashboard (2007–2008)
📌 Overview

This dashboard provides insights into jewellery production and sales data for the years 2007 and 2008. It helps analyze category-wise performance, quantity distribution, and comparison between diamond and stone quantities.

🎯 Key Metrics
Total Pieces: 44,052
Sum of Diamond Quantity (Dia Qty): 410,279
Sum of Stone Quantity: 79,183
📈 Dashboard Insights
1. Dia Qty vs CTS (2007–2008)
A pie chart showing the distribution of diamond quantity across different jewellery categories.
Categories include:
Bangles
Bracelets
Brooches
Clasps
Earrings
Necklaces
Pendants
Rings
Helps understand which category contributes most to diamond usage.
2. Total Pieces (2007–2008)
A bar chart comparing total jewellery pieces produced in 2007 vs 2008.
Shows category-wise production volume.
Useful for identifying high-production categories.
3. Stone Qty vs Dia Qty (2007–2008)
A line chart comparing stone quantity and diamond quantity.
Helps analyze trends and differences between stone and diamond usage across categories.
4. Stone Qty vs CTS (2007–2008)
A horizontal bar chart comparing stone quantity with casting totals.
Provides insights into production material usage.
5. Total Casting (2007–2008)
A donut chart showing total casting distribution across categories.
Helps visualize contribution of each category in total casting.
6. Dia Qty vs Stone Qty
A pie chart comparing overall diamond vs stone quantity.
Shows the proportion of materials used in production.
🛠 Tools Used
Microsoft Excel
Pivot Tables

Charts & Data Visualization
📂 Dataset Details
Data includes jewellery categories, production quantity, diamond quantity, and stone quantity.
Covers two years: 2007 and 2008.
Data was cleaned and transformed before visualization.
