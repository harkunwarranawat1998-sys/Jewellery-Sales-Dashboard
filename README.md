📊 Production Sales Dashboard (2007–2008)
📌 Overview

This dashboard presents a comprehensive analysis of jewellery production data for the years 2007 and 2008. It highlights key trends, category-wise performance, and material distribution, enabling better understanding of production patterns and business insights.

🎯 Key Metrics
Total Pieces Produced: 44,052
Total Diamond Quantity (Dia Qty): 410,279
Total Stone Quantity: 79,183
📈 Key Insights & Visualizations
1. Diamond Quantity vs Casting (Dia Qty vs CTS)

A pie chart illustrating the distribution of diamond quantity across various jewellery categories such as bangles, bracelets, earrings, necklaces, pendants, and rings.
👉 Helps identify which categories contribute most to diamond usage.

2. Total Pieces Produced (2007 vs 2008)

A clustered bar chart comparing total production volume across categories for both years.
👉 Useful for spotting growth trends and high-performing categories.

3. Stone Quantity vs Diamond Quantity

A line chart comparing stone and diamond quantities across categories.
👉 Highlights the relationship and variation between the two materials.

4. Stone Quantity vs Casting (CTS)

A horizontal bar chart showing how stone quantity aligns with casting totals.
👉 Provides insight into material utilization in production.

5. Total Casting Distribution

A donut chart representing total casting quantity segmented by jewellery categories.
👉 Clearly shows each category’s contribution to overall production.

6. Diamond vs Stone Quantity Share

A pie chart comparing the overall proportion of diamond and stone quantities.
👉 Helps understand the material composition of total production.
